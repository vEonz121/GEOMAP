Wellness Indices of Asian Countries, and the Happiest Countries. Data sourced from here and here.
(by Varun Yadav and Sustainable Development Solution Network)

Insight:

Based on the Overall Happiness Ranking, the top three countries are Japan, Singapore and Qatar.

The bottom three countries on the other hand are Bangladesh, Pakistan and Kazakhstan.

However, based on individual index rankings, Taiwanese residents enjoy the best healthcare despite ranking #5 overall. This does not reflect its ranking in Life Expectancy however, where Singaporeans are expected to live longest. Across these two indexes, Japan is a great option (#3 in both).

From the perspective of economic welfare, Qatar has the highest GDP with #6 in cost of living, making it an attractive investment country.

Qatar is also a good option for living welfare, ranking very high in both Quality of Life and Safety.
